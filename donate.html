Donate

It’s evident that today’s music industry opts for profit instead of culture and quality. There are tons of non-mainstream musicians whose music could have an amazing cultural and educational impact on the audience. They are just not exposed enough.

We are here to change that. Stellar Sound Podcast is a whole new Universe where music enthusiasts gather to exchange stories. It's a brilliant way of discovering new artists and extending your knowledge while having fun in the meantime. We incorporate a bunch of stimulating topics in our interviews so that we can make sure you’ll acquire lots of new information regarding the non-mainstream music world.

Still, our Universe can only continue expanding if you choose to be the boss and help us grow by contributing. You can do it by donating in one of the following ways:

Paypal donation

You’ll have the opportunity to be part of our ongoing mission to promote outstanding musicians. Thus, you’ll become one of the first lucky ones to join our community. Contributions in any amount are welcome, and indeed, are most gratefully received.

Patreon

If you are an artist, or you know one, you are surely familiar with the fact that artists don’t simply earn revenues from art only and often need support.

Our goal is to back up each other. We would absolutely love it if you were interested in becoming one of our first patrons and helping us develop. By joining us, you’ll not only support our project - you’ll also support a bunch of incredibly talented musicians we promote. Who knows, maybe one of them will become your favorite one day. ;)

Donate goods

Are your musical instruments collecting dust? Or are you maybe a good willed artist wanting to give away some of their work?
