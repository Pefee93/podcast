Stellar Streamed Episode 1

Guest: GAMMA | Host: Justen Williams

Gloriya a.k.a GAMMA is a musician from Bulgaria. Creativity and art in many forms are an output for her spirituality and phillosophy. In her country she is known with my jazzy melancholic covers of underground hip hop songs, however in her core she is a heartly performer for the live listener. I release a very minimal part of the music I create and I believe this has something to do with my understanding of intimacy between the live performer and the listener. I connect to the people and transform the music through a personal/group prism. This is something you cannot hear or see or feel online. I am energy sensitive and I have a strong connection to my gut (6th sense) which is not always a positive thing. My music online is a glimpse, a reflection of what I am, so this might be a hard matter to discuss. And I have other interviews online but they do not include my face, as I keep my identity private (excluding for my IG followers). I believe I am an artist with the mission to make my audience feel their connection to the common source through me. Too confusing? That is myself and I accept my exclusivity of the commerciality. Feel free to ask me anything.
