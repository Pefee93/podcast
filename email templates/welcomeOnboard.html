🌠 Better option
We recommend recording yourself with a quality microphone during the video call with your headphones on. This way you will isolate and record only your voice.

  • a) If you are recording audio and having the video call on the same device, please make sure those are not interfering with each other. 
  • b) If the video call is interfering with your audio recording, try having a video call via mobile and record audio on your PC/laptop;
 
🌠  Good option
If you don't have access to a quality audio setup, our suggestion is to use your mobile device as an audio recording device, while having a video call on your PC/laptop and your headphones on. Make sure you point the device towards your mouth and find out what is the optimal distance for a good recording; 
 
  • * We recommend testing this setup and making sure it works for you few days before the interview. Contact us for extra assistance. 
  • * We always recommend recording audio with another mobile device as an alternative source. The interview has only one shot!
