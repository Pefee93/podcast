who we are

What Is Stellar Sound Podcast?

The Stellar Sound Podcast is a podcast in English hosting guests from the European music industry.

The Stellar Sound Podcast brings you inspiration by interviewing the most creative, innovative, unique, eccentric, and ground-breaking musicians.

This is a never-stop-growing universe that encourages today's scene disruptors to grant their unique voice and enrich the music industry.

Vision

Our vision is a world that understands and appreciates true musicianship, innovation and diversity.

True creativity comes from authenticity and a time-perfected craft. With modern technology and accessibility, there is no better time to be a music artist.

Our final aim is uncovering special gems and making them accessible to anyone, no matter who you are.

what we do

Promote

It seems like today’s music industry’s focus is strictly on the mainstream. We are aware of the fact that there are many unknown artists and musicians out there that should be heard of. That’s why our goal is to put a spotlight on them and make sure they reach as large an audience as possible.

Educate

Every single artist we introduce in the podcast has a story to tell. We find it important that the audience has the opportunity to acquire new knowledge and broaden their horizons with every interview released.

Entertain

Our interviews are not just a bunch of serious story-telling moments. What’s more, you’ll have a lot of fun hearing witty remarks and comments, as well as spontaneous bloopers and jokes within different topics.

stellar team

The magic we find in other people's originality is the spark that is driving our team.

We are a group of musicians and music enthusiasts with a strong mission, having fun in the process.

We are based in Den Bosch, Utrecht, Niš, San Juan, Amsterdam, and Belgrade; working from home, connected by a universal mind.
