who we are

Stellar Sound Podcast is a podcast in English, connecting you to musicians, artists and other creatives, dedicated to inspire you.

We are an international group of music enthusiasts ourselves, with a mission to challenge each other and solve obstacles for creatives through our discussions and research... while having fun!

Based all over the globe; recording in the Netherlands.
