who we are

Stellar Sound Podcast is a podcast in English, connecting you to musicians, artists and other creatives, dedicated to inspire you.

We are an international group of music enthusiasts ourselves, with a mission to challenge each other and solve obstacles for creatives through our discussions and research... while having fun!

Based all over the globe; recording in the Netherlands.

what we do

The Serious Stuff

We poke and delve deeper into the artists’ journeys and their creations. At times we may get very personal and showcase just how many roadblocks our guests have had to overcome in pursuit of their realization.

The Silly Stuff

all the while we exchange witty insights (if lucky :)), create spontaneous bloopers and make something out of tiny failures along the way.

The Shiny Stuff

We are here to give our guests a humble share of the spotlight. Or the pod-light. Whatever a podcast-attention is called these days! We believe that art is worthy of more shares, and worthy of more heartbeats.
