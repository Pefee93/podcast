Episode 3

Guest: Aleksandra Denda

Our third episode guest is Aleksandra Denda, an internationally recognized performer, songwriter, music educator, and co-founder of a multinational vocal group ROSA.

In this chat with host Megan, Aleksandra is introspective about her experience of moving and living abroad for college, being an international musician, enduring one year of silence due to Covid, as well as balancing her two biggest passions, music education and performing.

Through the conversation, we get an insight into her mindset and how she keeps her inner peace during these times of worldwide musical stagnation, her EP ‘Dreamer’ and new single "Between The Lines".

  • CATEGORY: SINGER-SONGWRITER, VOCAL EDUCATOR, PERFORMER
  • RELEASED: 10/05/2021
  • LENGTH: 50 minutes
  • TOPICS: Living in New York & Being an international musician, Co-founding traditional serbian vocal group ROSA, Creative Process, Experience as a music educator, Teaching & Impact of Pandemic on Musicians, Collaborating from a Distance, New Single Release
